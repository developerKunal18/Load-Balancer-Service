🧠 Why This Is Important
Without a Load Balancer:
Client
   ↓
Server 1

If Server 1 fails:
❌ Application Down

With a Load Balancer:
          Client
             ↓
      Load Balancer
      ↙     ↓      ↘
 Server1 Server2 Server3

Benefits:
	•	✅ High Availability
	•	✅ Fault Tolerance
	•	✅ Scalability
	•	✅ Better Performance
